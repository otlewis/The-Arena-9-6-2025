Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my teen's account?

Email us at thearenadtd@gmail.com with your teen's name and account email. We'll process the deletion within 24 hours and confirm when it's complete. All personal data will be permanently removed.

What information do you collect about teen users?

We collect only the minimum necessary:

  • Account information (email, username, birth date)
  • Debate participation (posts, comments)
  • Voice/video during debates (not stored permanently)
  • Optional parent email (if provided)
  • Basic technical data (device type, IP for security)

We never use this data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

How do you ensure my teen's safety during debates?

Multiple safety layers protect your teen:

  • Human Moderators: Trained staff oversee all debates
  • Content Guidelines: Clear rules against harassment or inappropriate content
  • Reporting Tools: Easy one-click reporting of problems
  • Time Limits: Structured sessions prevent excessive usage
  • Educational Focus: Topics are curated for learning
Can I monitor my teen's activity on the platform?

Yes, as their parent/guardian you can:

  • Request a full report of their account activity
  • See what debates they've participated in
  • Review any content they've posted
  • Contact us for real-time concerns

Email thearenadtd@gmail.com to request an activity report.

What happens if policies are updated?

When we update our Terms of Service or Privacy Policy:

  • Your teen's account is temporarily restricted
  • You receive an email notification (if provided)
  • New consent is required before they can continue using the app
  • You can review all changes before deciding

This ensures you always know what you're agreeing to.

How do you handle cyberbullying or harassment?

Zero tolerance policy for harassment:

  • Immediate Action: Reported harassment is investigated within hours
  • Account Suspension: Violators lose access immediately
  • Parent Notification: All parents involved are contacted
  • Support Resources: We provide guidance on next steps

Your teen's safety is non-negotiable.

Is The Arena DTD free to use?

The Arena DTD offers both free and premium features. Teen users can participate in debates and discussions at no cost. Premium features may be available but are not required for the core educational experience.
