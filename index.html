🛡️ Arena App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 9, 2024

Quick Summary: Arena is a debate app that uses your microphone for voice chat and camera for optional video. We don't sell your data or store your conversations permanently.

📱 What is Arena?

Arena is a real-time debate and discussion platform where users engage in structured debates, open discussions, and challenge-based conversations.

📊 Information We Collect

Account Information

  • 📧 Email address (for login and notifications)
  • 👤 Username and display name
  • 📝 Optional profile information

Communication Data

  • 🎤 Voice chat: Live audio during debates (not recorded permanently)
  • 📹 Video: Optional camera use during debates
  • 💬 Text messages: Chat messages in discussion rooms
  • ⏱️ Participation: Room join/leave times and activity

🔧 How We Use Your Information

Core App Functions

  • 🎯 Enable real-time voice and video debates
  • 💬 Facilitate chat messaging in rooms
  • ⏰ Synchronize debate timers across all participants
  • 🏆 Track debate participation and scoring
  • 📢 Send notifications about challenges and room invites

🔐 Privacy & Security

🚫 We DO NOT:
  • ❌ Sell your personal information
  • ❌ Record or permanently store voice conversations
  • ❌ Share data with advertisers
  • ❌ Access your device outside the app

Data Protection

  • 🔒 All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • ☁️ Secure storage on Appwrite and Firebase servers
  • 🎤 Audio streams processed in real-time only
  • 💬 Chat messages stored for conversation history

Third-Party Services

We use these trusted services to make Arena work:

  • Appwrite: User accounts and database
  • Firebase: Real-time synchronization
  • LiveKit: Voice and video communication

📲 App Permissions Explained

Required Permissions

  • 🌐 Internet: Connect to servers for real-time communication
  • 🎤 Microphone: Voice chat during debates and discussions
  • 📶 Network State: Check connection quality

Optional Permissions

  • 📹 Camera: Video during debates (you control when to enable)
  • 🔵 Bluetooth: Connect wireless headphones/speakers
  • 🔔 Notifications: Alerts for challenges and messages

👤 Your Rights

  • 📄 Access: Request a copy of your data
  • 🗑️ Delete: Request account and data deletion
  • ✏️ Update: Modify your profile anytime
  • 🎛️ Control: Choose when to use camera/microphone

🧒 Children's Privacy

Arena is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

🗄️ Data Retention

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Chat messages: Stored for conversation continuity
  • Voice/video: Not permanently stored
  • Usage analytics: Anonymized for app improvement

📝 Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
